About Me

My name is Quinn Bruderer. I am a Junior student pursuing a BFA at the University Of Montana. I have declared a Media Arts major, with a Computer Science Minor. I have worked with many programs, including the Adobe Suite, Maya, and Unity. I am an active student in the Media Arts program, eagerly assisting other students. I am a TA for the CreativeCoding course, and have assisted other students learn JavaScript programming, and create interactive media.

When I have freetime (What is that?), I will often draw or create. Whether it is digitally produced or a physicial product, once I have an idea, I pursue making it. This includes customized LED Mirrors, Tapestry, Wood and Metalwork, as well as logos, characters and environments.

...now, I very much enjoy sitting down and watching a movie, or playing a game. Once that happens, who knows where time goes?

Story

I have a passion for the interactive technology envornment, and although I have times strayed away, it always has brought me back. The question that always excites me:
How do I make something good...Something Great?

I am unsure which franchise came first (due to a 1 week gap), however I was introduced into the gaming industry with two very iconic franchises. Spyro The Dragon & Pokemon. I continued over the years with many other franchises. At 7th grade I was able to get my hands on Adobe Photoshop, and created collages and basic effects. Once I learned there was required logic to this artform, I proceeded to learn programming fundimentals.

Since then, I have worked with Adobe Premiere, Audition, Photoshop, Illustrator, and favorably, After Effects.
