Story

I have a passion for the interactive technology envornment, and although I have times strayed away, it always has brought me back. The question that always excites me:
How do I make something good...Something Great?

I am unsure which franchise came first (due to a 1 week gap), however I was introduced into the gaming industry with two very iconic franchises. Spyro The Dragon & Pokemon. I continued over the years with many other franchises. At 7th grade I was able to get my hands on Adobe Photoshop, and created collages and basic effects. Once I learned there was required logic to this artform, I proceeded to learn programming fundimentals.

Since then, I have worked with Adobe Premiere, Audition, Photoshop, Illustrator, and favorably, After Effects.
